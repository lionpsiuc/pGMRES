\section{The Arnoldi Iteration}

We present the pseudocode of the Arnoldi iteration algorithm exactly (without the early termination given some level of tolerance) as is given in \citet{arnoldi2025lab}:

\begin{algorithm}[H]
    \begin{algorithmic}
        \Procedure{arnoldi}{$A,u,m$}
        \State$n\gets\text{length}(u)$
        \State$Q\gets\text{zeros}(n,m+1)$\Comment{Initialise matrix to hold orthonormal basis vectors}
        \State$H\gets\text{zeros}(m+1,m)$\Comment{Initialise Hessenberg matrix}
        \State$Q[:,0]\gets u/\|u\|_2$
        \For{$j=0$ to $m-1$}
        \State$Q[:,j+1]\gets A\cdot Q[:,j]$
        \For{$i=0$ to $j$}
        \State$H[i,j]\gets\langle Q[:,i],Q[:,j+1]\rangle$
        \State$Q[:,j+1]\gets Q[:,j+1]-H[i,j]\cdot Q[:,i]$\Comment{Orthogonalise against $Q[:,i]$}
        \EndFor
        \State$H[j+1,j]\gets\|Q[:,j+1]\|_2$
        \State$Q[:,j+1]\gets Q[:,j+1]/H[j+1,j]$
        \EndFor
        \State\Return$Q,H$
        \EndProcedure
    \end{algorithmic}
\end{algorithm}
